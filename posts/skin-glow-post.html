Daily Skincare Routine

Skincare is all about nurturing your skin, so it stays healthy, balanced, and radiant. It involves simple daily habits—like cleansing, moisturizing, and protecting with sunscreen—that help support your skins natural barrier and keep it looking its best. With the right routine, skincare becomes less about perfection and more about caring for yourself in a way that feels good and sustainable. Its to show off your glowy healthy skin to the world.

What a morning skincare routine looks like:

Step 1: Wash your face with a gentle cleanser (remove overnight oil, its a refresh)

Step 2: Add a hydrating toner

Step 3: Add serum: ^Vitamin C (brightness and protection) ^Hyaluronic acid (hydration) ^Niacinamide (pores & oil balance) .

Step 4: Add moisturizer (locks in hydration & healthy skin barrier)

Step 5: Add sunscreen *IMPORTANT* (30 or higher SPF)

What does a daily wash day look like on curly hair:

Step 1: Use shampoo twice (find shampoo that says clarifying; to remove build up products you use for your hair)

Step 2: Use conditioner (ONLY for your ends). While having conditioner on your hair, gently start brushing through your hair to lock moisturizer.

Step 3: Use leave-in conditioner after you shower.

Step 4: Add gel and use your styling brush to get the curls.

Step 5: Add mouse to hold in the curls and start scrunching your hair.

Step 6: Apply hair protection spray.

Step 7: Start diffusing your hair with a cool setting not hot.

Step 8: Apply hair oil to break the gel cast.
