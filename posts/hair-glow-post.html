A Guide to Wash Days

Daily wash day is your little reset moment, a fresh start for your hair. Begin with a gentle shampoo that lifts away buildup without stealing your shine. Melt in a hydrating conditioner smoothly and soften every strand. Rinse with warm water, detangle slowly, and finish with a light leave-in or hair oil for that natural glow. This simple ritual keeps your hair clean, nourished, and effortlessly beautiful—every single day.

What does a daily wash day look like on straight/wavy hair

Step 1: Use shampoo twice (Find shampoos that says for "shiny, volume, or moisturizing). Let the shampoo sit for at least two minutes.

Step 2: Use conditioner (Only for your ends never for your roots). While having conditioner you can start brushing thoroughly and gently to lock in that moisturizer.

Step 3: Add leave-in conditioner (only for your hair not the roots of it).

Step 4: Add heat protector to blow dry your hair

Step 5: Add scalp serums (optional) Its for hair growth.

Step 6: Once your hair is all dry you can add your hair oil.

What does a daily wash day look like on curly hair:

Step 1: Use shampoo twice (find shampoo that says clarifying; to remove build up products you use for your hair)

Step 2: Use conditioner (ONLY for your ends). While having conditioner on your hair, gently start brushing through your hair to lock moisturizer.

Step 3: Use leave-in conditioner after you shower.

Step 4: Add gel and use your styling brush to get the curls.

Step 5: Add mouse to hold in the curls and start scrunching your hair.

Step 6: Apply hair protection spray.

Step 7: Start diffusing your hair with a cool setting not hot.

Step 8: Apply hair oil to break the gel cast.
